Privacy Policy

WhoopBro · Last updated: March 7, 2026

1. What We Collect

WhoopBro collects the following data to provide its service:

  • Telegram account info: Your Telegram user ID, first name, and username (if public).
  • WHOOP health data: Recovery score, HRV, resting heart rate, SpO₂, sleep data (duration, stages, efficiency), strain score, and caloric data — fetched daily via the WHOOP API after you authorize access.
  • App preferences: Your preferred language and daily brief delivery time.
  • Payment info: Telegram Stars transaction IDs (we do not store card numbers or financial details).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Generate your personalized daily health brief and recommendations.
  • Answer your on-demand health questions.
  • Manage your subscription status.

We do not sell, share, or use your health data for advertising or any purpose beyond providing the WhoopBro service.

3. Data Retention

Daily health snapshots are retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted. You can request full deletion at any time via the /disconnect command, which immediately removes all your tokens and health data from our systems.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your WHOOP OAuth tokens are stored encrypted (AES-256-GCM) in our database. Access tokens are never logged. Our servers are hosted on Hetzner Cloud (EU).

5. Third-Party Services

  • WHOOP API — health data source. Subject to WHOOP's Privacy Policy.
  • Telegram — messaging platform. Subject to Telegram's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API — AI model for generating recommendations. Health data is sent to Gemini for processing but not stored by Google beyond the API call.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all your data at any time via /disconnect in the bot.
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us.
  • Revoke WHOOP access from your WHOOP app's connected apps settings.

7. Medical Disclaimer

WhoopBro provides general wellness information only. Nothing in the service constitutes medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions.
